Integrity Officers (DVN) Doctrine

Mandate: Human-in-the-Loop claims arbitration. Mandated by NY law to review and execute any adverse claim denials.

Decentralized Validator Network (DVN): Institutional nodes utilize Agent Identity NFTs bound to W3C Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs) to verify KYB and regulatory capabilities.

Responsibilities: High-margin, Human-In-The-Loop (HITL) auditing for edge cases and SIU escalations to prevent fraudulent behavior from executing smart contracts.
