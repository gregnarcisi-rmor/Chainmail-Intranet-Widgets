  • Deliver physical event trigger audits following any parametric catastrophe that impacts policy limits.

3. Reporting & Documentation Requirements

  • Oversee and sign off on the annual ITV Deficit Reconciliation and Rate Math baseline audits.
  • Present official National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC) compliance reports.
  • Review and validate Form, Rate, and Market Conduct Authorization filings submitted to state insurance regulators (e.g., Florida OIR).

4. Human-Agent Symbiosis
