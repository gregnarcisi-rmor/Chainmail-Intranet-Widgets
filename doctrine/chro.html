Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO) Doctrine

Human Lead: Crystal Settles

Assigned Co-Pilot: Identity_Access_Manager

1. Executive Mandate

Oversee global talent acquisition, organizational structure design, human resources policy compliance, onboarding programs, and credentials authentication. Direct the development of Chainmail's human-agent symmetrical workflows.

2. Key Responsibilities

  • Direct and administer secure team credentialing keys and manage credentials authentication checklists.
  • Update and maintain the official corporate roster, ensuring clear distinction between onboarded staff and pipeline candidates.
  • Audit Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) security groups and verify active access permissions.
  • Coordinate the development of training curriculum and caseworker guidelines with Learning & Development specialists.

3. Reporting & Compliance

  • Conduct monthly access log security reviews and report unauthorized credentialing events to the CTO.
  • Maintain onboarding compliance records, verifying background checks and contract sign-offs for new team members.
  • Publish quarterly organizational health reports and team development plans to the CEO.

4. Human-Agent Symbiosis

Collaborates with the Identity_Access_Manager to authorize security keys, enforce cryptographic verification protocols, and run continuous audits on user credentials logs.
