Chief Experience Officer (CXO) Doctrine Tentative / Pipeline

Human Lead: Elizabeth Bullard (Tentative)

Assigned Co-Pilot: Product Consumer Agent

1. Executive Mandate

Direct the end-to-end customer experience, onboarding journeys, and physical expansion of the Device Verification Network (DVN). Oversee the local installer networks to ensure seamless setup and high-fidelity data connections for all policyholders.

2. Key Responsibilities

  • Coordinate the DVN validator installation pipelines and mapping of physical weather stations.
  • QA test the Customer Application Interface from a layperson's perspective using simulated end-user scenarios.
  • Identify UX friction points in onboarding, billing, and claims, simplifying parametric coverage explanations.
  • Monitor new customer activation velocity and verify device connection SLA timelines.

3. Reporting & Metrics Oversight

  • Track user activation rates, DVN drive-by verification timelines, and customer satisfaction (CSAT) scores.
  • Audit the status of unverified nodes and validator dispatch pipelines in the hardware registry.
  • Review feedback reports and friction logs generated by the Product Consumer Agent.

4. Human-Agent Symbiosis

Collaborates with the Product Consumer Agent to analyze simulated focus group feedback, test new mobile app releases, and automatically log experience improvements (UX-ENHANCEMENT tickets) directly to Jira.
