Human Lead: Jeremy Metts

Assigned Co-Pilot: LOI Converter

1. Executive Mandate

Drive corporate sales operations, relationship building, marketing campaigns, and retail agent distribution networks. Standardize the acquisition channels for wholesale and retail insurance brokers joining the parametric network.

2. Key Responsibilities

  • Convert incoming broker Letters of Intent (LOIs) into active onboarded profiles.
  • Coordinate marketing collateral, retail branding campaigns, and community engagement.

  • Audit wholesale broker pipeline growth and contract conversions.

3. Reporting & Campaign Auditing

  • Monitor LOI conversion velocities and campaign ROI metrics.
  • Present monthly broker enrollment logs and channel acquisition cost audits.
  • Coordinate community expansion strategies across Web3 and insurance sectors.

4. Human-Agent Symbiosis

Collaborates with the LOI Converter agent to automatically parse broker onboarding forms, generate pre-filled contract templates, and track onboarding pipelines.
