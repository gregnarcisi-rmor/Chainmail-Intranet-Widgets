Chief Financial Officer (CFO) Doctrine

Human Lead: Samantha McKinney

Assigned Co-Pilots: budget_automator & liquidity_ledger

1. Executive Mandate

Manage corporate financial accounts, balance sheets, capital reserves, tax compliance, and treasury ledger allocations. Oversee the PGA and Natural Life structured insurance ledgers, maintaining fiscal health and reinsurance capacity.

2. Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor operational burn rate and enforce daily spending limits to maintain the bootstrapped spending directive.
  • Reconcile the daily spends ledger with real-time Google Sheets inputs.
  • Coordinate state-level tax allocations (5.0% + 0.06% Florida; 3.6% + 0.15% New York) for all parametric contracts.
  • Audit active reinsurance retention thresholds and attachment points.

3. Reporting & Auditing

  • Compile and sign off on quarterly balance sheet audits and cash flow reconciliations.
  • Maintain security and audit reports for escrow lockbox reserves and yield accounts (4.85% APY Slate).
  • Present daily operating liquidity metrics and reserve compliance reports.

4. Human-Agent Symbiosis

Collaborates with the budget_automator to sync expenditures and trigger notifications when close to quota thresholds, and the liquidity_ledger to verify locked capital reserves.
