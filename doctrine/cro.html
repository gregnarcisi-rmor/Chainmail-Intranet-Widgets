Chief Risk Officer (CRO) Doctrine

Mandate: Enterprise risk, regulatory compliance (SLIP+/ELANY), and solvency capital preservation. Leads DOAH/Audit defense.

SOC 2 Readiness: Mandated to track and prepare formal IT policies (Incident Response, Disaster Recovery) and schedule third-party smart contract penetration testing.

Responsibilities: Ensures all parametric transactions are mapped to proper coverage codes and oversees the EL-1 Bordereau reporting requirements for New York.
