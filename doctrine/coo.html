Chief Operating Officer (COO) Doctrine

Mandate: Day-to-day scaling, vendor relations, and infrastructure optimization. Manages the transition to full-time status.

Pillar Operations: Includes Directors of Business & AI Operations, Customer Experience, Legal/Risk/Compliance, and Corporate Services.

Responsibilities: Ensures the $0.00 Bootstrapped Spend mandate is upheld. Oversees the IAM Architecture and the integration of Human-Agent Symmetry.
