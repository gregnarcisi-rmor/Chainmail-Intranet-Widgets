Chief of Insurance & Claims (COI) Doctrine

Human Lead: Michael Barnett

Assigned Co-Pilot: Vigilance Shield

1. Executive Mandate

Direct all daily insurance claims operations, adjustment verification procedures, forensic meteorology telemetry audits, and Special Investigation Unit (SIU) fraud prevention protocols.

2. Key Responsibilities

  • Oversee claims routing thresholds and validate weather telemetry anomalies (windspeed, precipitation, pressure triggers).
  • Review and resolve claim flags involving potential data anomalies, sensor geofencing discrepancies, or telemetry alterations.
  • Audit forensic meteorology logs and hurricane track models following catastrophe triggers.
  • Provide the necessary human-in-the-loop (HITL) authorization for claim denials or high-value settlement releases.

3. Reporting & Metrics Oversight

  • Review real-time fraud metrics, ingestion data logs, and telemetry discrepancy flags.
  • Compile and sign off on post-catastrophe claims audits and forensic weather summary briefs.
  • Audit SIU escalation metrics and ensure regulatory compliance with claims adjustment standards.

4. Human-Agent Symbiosis

Collaborates with the Vigilance Shield agent to automatically flag anomalies, evaluate photo evidence coordinates, and route verified claims to the paymaster reserves.
